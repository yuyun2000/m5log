| `M5.Display` 是 M5Unified 提供的主显示对象，本质是一个 `M5GFX` 实例，用来做所有屏幕绘图操作。[[Basic usage](https://docs.m5stack.com/en/github/M5Unified/2.3-basic-usage)]<br><br>下面按常用“参数/方法”简单分组说明（只列出知识源里明确提到的）：<br><br>### 1. 基本文本相关<br><br>- `setTextSize(size)`：设置文字缩放倍数（浮点或整数，1 为默认大小）。[[Basic usage](https://docs.m5stack.com/en/github/M5Unified/2.3-basic-usage)]<br>- `setTextColor(color)`：设置文字前景色。[[Basic usage](https://docs.m5stack.com/en/github/M5Unified/2.3-basic-usage)]<br>- `setCursor(x, y)`：设置文本光标位置。[[Basic usage](https://docs.m5stack.com/en/github/M5Unified/2.3-basic-usage)]<br>- `print()/println()`：按当前光标位置输出文本。[[Basic usage](https://docs.m5stack.com/en/github/M5Unified/2.3-basic-usage)]<br>- `drawString(text, x, y)`：在指定坐标绘制字符串。[[Nesso N1 display](https://docs.m5stack.com/en/arduino/arduino_nesso_n1/display)]<br><br>扩展文本控制（在 Cardputer 文档中示例，同样属于 M5GFX 能力）：<br><br>- `setTextFont(&font)`：设置字体（如 `&fonts::Orbitron_Light_32`）。<br>- `setTextDatum(datum)`：设置对齐基准（如 `middle_center`）。[[Cardputer text](https://docs.m5stack.com/en/github/M5Unified/2.3-basic-usage)]<br><br>### 2. 屏幕配置与属性<br><br>- `setRotation(0–3)`：设置屏幕方向（0/1/2/3）。[[Nesso N1 display](https://docs.m5stack.com/en/arduino/arduino_nesso_n1/display)]<br>- `setBrightness(0–255)`：设置背光亮度。[[Getting started](https://docs.m5stack.com/en/github/M5Unified/2-getting-started)]<br>- `width()` / `height()`：获取当前方向下的宽高像素。[[Cardputer display](https://docs.m5stack.com/en/github/M5Unified/2.3-basic-usage)]<br><br>### 3. 画图与清屏<br><br>- `fillScreen(color)` / `clear()`：用指定颜色清屏。[[Basic 填充矩形。[[Basic usage](https://docs.m5stack.com/en/github/M5Unified/2.3-basic-usage)]<br>- `drawCircle(x, y, r, color)` / `fillCircle(x, y, r, color)`：画圆 / 填充圆。[[Basic usage](https://docs.m5stack.com/en/github/M5Unified/2.3-basic-usage)]<br><br>### 4. 多显示与别名<br><br>- `M5.Display`：主显示对象（`M5GFX` 实例）。<br>- `M5.Lcd`：`M5.Display` 的别名，功能完全相同。[[Basic
